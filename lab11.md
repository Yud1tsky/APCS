# Theme: Cats!
## Adventurer type 1: Defense cat
Defense cats will have the following properties:
  1. Increased base HP
  2. Weak attack function
  3. Special attack - reduce the damage done by the next attack to its own team by a random value between 20 and 50%
## Adventurer type 2: Battle cat
Battle cats will have the following properties:
  1. Normal base HP
  2. Strong attack function
  3. Special attack - deal a total damage value to the opposing team, distributed among the remaining adventurers
## Adventurer type 3: Support cat
Support cats will have the following properties:
  1. Decreased base HP
  2. Ability to heal one player on its own team
  3. Special ability - heal entire remaining team for 2 turns and buff next attack
## Boss
The boss will have the following properties:
  1. 4x the normal base HP of a battle cat
  2. Deal splash attacks with targeted damage on one player and lingering damage over 3 turns to the others, decided as a random percent from 1-30 of their remaining HP
